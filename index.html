Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our auto detailing services

We recommend a basic exterior wash every 2 weeks, interior cleaning every 1-3 months, and a full detail every 6-12 months depending on your vehicle usage and environmental conditions. Ceramic coatings typically last 2-5 years before needing reapplication.

Service times vary based on the package: Express Clean (15-30 minutes), Basic Detail (2-3 hours), Premium Detail (4-5 hours), and Ceramic Coating (1-2 days). Paint correction and extensive interior work may require additional time.

Our interior detailing includes vacuuming of all surfaces, deep cleaning of carpets and upholstery, leather conditioning, dashboard and console cleaning, window cleaning, odor elimination, and thorough cleaning of vents, cup holders, and other hard-to-reach areas.

Absolutely! Ceramic coating provides long-lasting protection against UV damage, chemical stains, bird droppings, and environmental contaminants. It creates a hydrophobic surface that makes washing easier and maintains your vehicle's shine for years. The initial investment saves you money on frequent washes and paint correction in the long run.

Yes, we're committed to environmental sustainability. We use biodegradable, water-based products that are safe for your vehicle and the environment. Our waterless wash option conserves hundreds of gallons of water per vehicle compared to traditional methods.

Yes, our paint correction services effectively remove light to moderate scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation. For deeper scratches, we may recommend touch-up paint before polishing. We use advanced techniques and professional-grade compounds to restore your paint's clarity and depth.

Please remove personal belongings, especially valuables. For interior detailing, try to clear floor mats and storage compartments. For exterior work, we recommend bringing your car in as clean as possible, but we can handle heavily soiled vehicles too. No other preparation is needed!

Yes, we offer mobile detailing for an additional fee. Our fully equipped van comes to your home or office with everything needed for a complete detail.
